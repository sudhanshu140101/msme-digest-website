ABOUT THE AUTHOR

Mukesh Mohan Gupta

President – Chamber of Indian Micro Small and Medium Enterprises

Mukesh Mohan Gupta is a Chartered Accountant by Profession, having more than 30 years of experience in Banking, Finance, MSMEs, Economy, Law Accounting and Auditing.

He has served on the Board of Dena Bank as an Independent Director. He was also appointed on the board of one of the TATA Group Company and other Public Sector Undertakings by BIFR, Ministry of Finance, Government of India.

He was also nominated as a member on the National Board of MSME, Ministry of Micro Small & Medium Enterprises.

He was invited by the Hon’ble Prime Minister of India Shri Narendra Modi ji at his official residence to discuss the current economic situation and way forward especially for MSMEs, in a small group.

He also operates a YouTube channel called “MSME HELPLINE”, wherein he has posted more than 800 videos on various schemes for MSMEs. The channel has a subscriber base of more than 1 Million and viewership of more than 50 millions.

He has authored a book "MSMEs of Developed India- An Encyclopaedia of MSME Schemes" covering all schemes related to MSMEs of all Central Government Ministries and other Stakeholders. The book was launched by Hon’ble Minister of Commerce and Industry Sh Piyush Goyal Ji.

He also hosted a "Positive Talk with Mukesh Mohan Gupta" Show on ZEE BUSINESS and covered many Central Cabinet Ministers, Dy Governors RBI and many CMDs / EDs of Banks.
